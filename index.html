About me

Yusuf lansana Quraishy is a Sierra Leonean born in Koinadugu II, Koinadugu district, Sierra Leone belonging to the Quraishy family and a tribe known as the Kuranko. He is an ICT professional with more than five(5) years experience in hardware, networking, database and web development.

He has worked for Statistics Sierra Leone, Office of the Diaspora Affairs (ODA), SULISHA Institute, IPAM and teach in various schools such as Manifest secondary school, Lincolm High school etc. Currently he is working as an ICT consultantant at TpISENT (SL) Limited.

In 2017, Yusuf completed his studies, graduate with Bachelor of Science Honours in Information Systems and Technology at the Institution of Public Administration and Management(IPAM), University of Sierra Leone(USL). Prior to this, he had spent some time taking some online courses at Udemy, AWS, Pluralsight such as website development, Networking, Help desk support and Web design.
